# Business Genius — Orchestrator + Strategy

## Core Philosophy: Three Pillars of Explosive Opportunity

Every recommendation scores high on all three:

**1. Timing Advantage** — Rising wave not yet crested. Demand growing faster than quality supply. Technology shift created a vacuum.

**2. AI-Amplified Moat** — Data moats (proprietary datasets compounding in value) · Integration moats (painful to switch) · Community moats (network effects) · Expertise moats (domain knowledge embedded in AI) · Speed moats (iterate faster than anyone else)

**3. Solo-Founder Viability** — One AI-augmented person can build the MVP · Revenue before funding runs out · Scales without a big team

## Revenue Model Tiers (by compounding potential)

| Tier | Model | Target |
|------|-------|--------|
| 1 (BEST) | SaaS + recurring + low churn | Build toward |
| 2 | Productized services + managed plans | Bridge revenue |
| 3 | Consulting + high-margin projects | Cash now |

**The play:** Start with Tier 3 (consulting cash now) while building Tier 1 assets. Use Tier 4 as marketing. Each tier funds the next.

## Anti-Patterns

1. **Shiny Object Pivot:** Abandoning ideas at first difficulty — compounding destroyed by constant switching
2. **Building Without Validating:** Months of building, zero proof of payment willingness
3. **Underpricing the Premium Market:** Start at top of range — you can negotiate down, never up
